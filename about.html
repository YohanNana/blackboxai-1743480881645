About V-Maint

The smarter way to track and manage your vehicle maintenance

Our Story

Founded in 2023, V-Maint was created by car enthusiasts who were frustrated with the complexity of tracking vehicle maintenance. We wanted a simple, intuitive solution that would help people take better care of their vehicles.

Today, V-Maint helps thousands of vehicle owners stay on top of maintenance, reduce repair costs, and extend the life of their cars, trucks, and motorcycles.

Key Features

Maintenance Reminders

Never miss an oil change or service interval with our smart reminder system.

Advanced Analytics

Track maintenance costs and patterns with our detailed analytics dashboard.

AI Predictions

Our AI predicts when you'll need service based on your driving patterns.
